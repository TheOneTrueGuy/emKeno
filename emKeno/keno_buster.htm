Keno Buster

 

        Keno Buster is an advanced neural net program designed to discern patterns in keno sequences and any other computer generated random numbers. Keno Buster runs on most pocket pc type handheld PDAs.

Keno buster has a simple 10 key pad for entering numbers which appear in the top cells. When all 20 numbers have been entered there are three different methods for detecting patterns, depending on how you want to do it. Scan is the fastest, simplest method. It doesn't require that numbers be entered in any particular order. In Order is the most accurate method but it requires that numbers be entered as they come out of the keno generator (before they've been sorted.) this method is easiest if you have someone to tell you the numbers. If you're alone and slow like me, you can use Sorted to look for patterns after the keno numbers have been sorted from small to large. It's less acute at picking out patterns but still gains a 12% advantage.
